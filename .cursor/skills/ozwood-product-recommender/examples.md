# Recommendation examples

Examples use catalog fields from `data/products.json`. Prices shown are illustrative of the volatile catalog snapshot — always re-check the `source` page before quoting.

---

## 1. Pets + waterproof + tight budget

**Persona:** apartment · living · bright · cool · pets · kids-pets · waterproof · under35

**Hard filter:** `fit.moisture === waterproof`, prefer `fit.budget === under35`, style cool/light.

**Primary direction:** Hybrid Grey Oak (or similar cool hybrid on sale) — waterproof + AC4 + budget band.

**Trade-off:** Looks like timber but is not a real oak wear layer; confirm current promo price on the product page.

**Alternates:** Other waterproof hybrids in grey/light tones; waterproof laminate only if tagged waterproof.

**Next step:** Order a sample; confirm kitchen-edge sealing on site.

---

## 2. Dim room + light / brighten

**Persona:** house · bedroom · dim · light · none · quiet · occasional · 35-55

**Hard filter:** Avoid forcing waterproof-only. Prefer `fit.style` with `light` and lighting including `dim`.

**Primary direction:** Champagne / white / light oak laminate or light hybrid in budget band.

**Trade-off:** Laminate brightens well and controls cost; less “real timber” than engineered oak.

**Alternates:** Light engineered oak if budget can move to quote/55plus for authenticity.

---

## 3. Australian hardwood character

**Persona:** house · living · bright · australian · none · quiet · dry · quote

**Hard filter:** `fit.moisture` dry OK; style `australian` / species Spotted Gum, Blackbutt, Jarrah.

**Primary direction:** Engineered or solid Spotted Gum / Blackbutt from catalog with `personaHints` 澳洲硬木个性.

**Trade-off:** Natural colour variation — view multiple boards; not a wet-area floor.

**Alternates:** Australian-look hybrid if they later add pets/moisture needs (explain it is a look-alike structure).

---

## 4. Herringbone feature living room

**Persona:** house · living · bright · herringbone · none · quiet · dry · 55plus or quote

**Hard filter:** `fit.style` includes herringbone / type parquetry; warn labour & waste.

**Primary direction:** Engineered Oak Herringbone (e.g. 4301 family) or listed parquetry SKU.

**Trade-off:** Higher install skill, waste, and setting-out; budget and timeline rise.

**Alternates:** Straight-lay warm engineered oak if they want timber warmth without pattern complexity.

**Next step:** Showroom layout discussion + site measure before firm quote.

---

## 5. Insufficient evidence

**User:** “What floor is best?”

**Do not** pick a random SKU.

**Do:** Explain hybrid vs engineered at type level in one short contrast, then ask the highest-value missing slot (usually moisture or household/pets, then budget/style).
