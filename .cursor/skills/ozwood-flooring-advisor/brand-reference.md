# Ozwood brand reference

Verified against the public website on 2026-07-19.

## Brand essence

- Official themes: “Our Expertise, Your Confidence”; craftsmanship meeting the natural elegance of timber; quality materials and careful detailing; elegant and enduring finishes.
- Ozwood describes timber as more than a building material: a natural product shaped by time and valued for character, strength, and beauty.
- The desired home outcome is comfortable and considered, with each step reflecting timber's natural character.

## Company and service

- The About page says the business began as Golden Elephant Pty Ltd, founded by Wayne Wu in 1988, and established its Australian presence in 2008.
- Product scope includes engineered, solid timber, laminate, hybrid, parquetry, stairs, feature walls, and accessories.
- Service themes include curated selection and supply, trained staff, careful installation, clean detailing, and helping customers visualise options in showrooms.
- The site says Ozwood has served more than 10,000 Sydney families. Treat counters and ratings as time-sensitive marketing facts.

## Design language

These are informed design interpretations, not fixed company promises:

- Light oak and restrained grain: airy, calm, suited to lower-light or compact interiors.
- Warm natural timber: comfortable, enduring, and broadly compatible with family homes.
- Australian hardwood: expressive grain and natural colour variation with a local character.
- Herringbone/parquetry: heritage, precision, directional movement, and a more formal focal point.
- Cool grey: restrained, contemporary, and suitable for minimal or some commercial interiors.

## Advice boundaries

- Selection should consider space, light, household use, moisture, subfloor, maintenance expectations, budget, and installation scope.
- Engineered wood offers a real timber surface and improved structural stability but still needs moisture care.
- Laminate is generally wear-resistant and budget-friendly; hybrid generally prioritises water resistance and easy living. Product-specific documentation controls.
- Check substrate stability, flatness, and moisture before installation. Herringbone requires precise setting-out and usually greater labour and waste.
- Never convert “moisture resistant” into “waterproof” or “waterproof” into permission for indefinite standing water.
